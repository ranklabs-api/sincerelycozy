Frequently Asked Questions

What are your swaddles made of?

95% rayon from bamboo and 5% spandex for softness plus stretch.

When should I stop swaddling?

When your baby shows signs of rolling, transition to arms-out sleep for safety.

Do you ship outside the U.S.?

At this time, Sincerely Cozy ships within the U.S. only.
