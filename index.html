From a Mom to Another

Hi, I’m Palloma. I created Sincerely Cozy after struggling to find swaddles that were both easy to use and genuinely soft.

I wanted safer-feeling comfort, timeless prints, and practical quality for real newborn life. Every swaddle is designed with that mission in mind: help parents feel confident and help babies feel cozy.
